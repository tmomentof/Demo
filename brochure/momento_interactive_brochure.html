Each interpretation is decomposed into weighted sub-claims, scored across Resonate, Contradict, and Diverge independently for thought and feeling,
then ranked against other readers using a reinforcement learning model that improves with every interaction.

01 · Decomposing Agent
The Interpreter
gemini-2.5-flash

Breaks each reader's interpretation into weighted sub-claims, each assigned an emotional mode.

breaking down the momentos
02 · Compatibility Agent
The Assessor
gemini-2.5-flash

For each matched sub-claim pair, Think and Feel questions are answered, each contributing to a final Resonate, Contradict, or Diverge score.

think + feel · per pair scoring
03 · Aggregator
The Scribe
pure python

Combines all sub-claim scores into final think/feel R/C/D percentages + dominant result + confidence per reader pair.

weighted avg · dominant R/C/D · confidence
04 · Ranking
reinforcement learning
Final Score = (compatibility × w₁) + (reinforcement × w₂)

New users rank purely on compatibility. Reinforcement weight starts at zero. As users explore, wave, and connect, weights update automatically to surface the readers who matter most.

new user → w₁ = 1.0 · w₂ = 0.0 · weights update with every interaction

Two Hands Reaching

The upright t is Think, the analytical dimension of interpretation. The inverted ʃ is Feel, how a passage lands in the body. Together they form two hands reaching toward the same book from opposite sides. The logo and the hero photograph are the same moment in two different forms. One literal. One geometric.

2
The Capture Frame

The t and ʃ together form a rectangle of negative space at their centre. That rectangle is the passage waiting to be captured. When a reader snips a passage in Read, a frame appears around the selected text; that frame and the negative space inside the logo are the same shape. The logo contains the gesture that begins every Momento.
